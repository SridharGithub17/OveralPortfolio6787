Deal evaluation framework

  • 1% rule: Monthly rent ≥ 1% of purchase price (Ohio cash-flow markets often clear this).
  • Cap rate: NOI ÷ price. Target 8%+ for Ohio buy-and-hold.
  • Cash-on-cash: Annual cash flow ÷ cash invested. Target 10%+.
  • GRM (gross rent multiplier): Price ÷ annual rent. Lower is better; 6-9 is solid.
  • Expense ratio: Operating expenses typically 35-45% of gross rent ex-mortgage.
  • Neighborhood class: Tier A/B/C — Cleveland/Toledo cash-flow neighborhoods often C; underwrite vacancy higher.
  • Reserves: Hold 3-6 months of mortgage + 1% of property value annually for capex.
Score = 40·(cap÷target) + 40·(CoC÷target) + 20·(rent÷price÷1%). Capped 100.
